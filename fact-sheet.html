How the portfolio is run

Three funds. One decision — hold them, and rebalance to policy weights when drift exceeds five percentage points.

The core is a broad global equity index. Around it, two sleeves tilt toward smaller and more profitably-priced companies. History suggests that combination compounds a little faster than the market alone, at the cost of tracking noise over short windows.
